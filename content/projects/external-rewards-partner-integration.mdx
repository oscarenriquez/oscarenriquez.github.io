## Context

External integrations become high-risk when event volume grows or partner systems behave unpredictably.

This project focused on building a robust cross-platform event pipeline that could support reward scenarios without creating fragile coupling.

## Impact

- Enabled reward workflows based on external interaction events
- Expanded cross-platform marketing and engagement capabilities
- Improved scalability and operational robustness for partner-driven processing

## Contribution

I worked directly on the integration design and implementation, helping shape a scalable event-driven flow that could support partner collaboration without exposing internal platform details.
