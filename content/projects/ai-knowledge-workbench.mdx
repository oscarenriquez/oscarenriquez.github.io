## Context

The value of AI in engineering teams comes from reducing search cost without increasing hallucination risk.

That means architecture matters more than prompt cleverness.

## Architecture

Content ingestion normalized source material, indexing made retrieval predictable, and the generation layer stayed grounded in retrieved evidence. The important design choice was keeping these boundaries explicit.

## Trade-offs

Retrieval quality depends on source quality. A weak corpus cannot be fixed downstream by a stronger model, so the implementation treated curation and evaluation as first-class responsibilities.
