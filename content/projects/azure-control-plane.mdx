## Context

A senior platform contribution is often less about a single feature and more about reducing friction for every future feature.

This project focused on turning cloud deployment into a disciplined product surface rather than an ad hoc collection of scripts.

## Architecture

The baseline included infrastructure as code, deployment workflows, and environment-aware configuration boundaries. Application teams could ship without re-solving release engineering each time.

## Trade-offs

Centralized platform patterns can drift into over-control. The design kept the baseline opinionated where operational risk was high, while leaving room for application-level autonomy.
