Payments engineering teaches a useful instinct: any system that affects trust needs clear boundaries, observable behavior, and predictable failure handling.

AI systems benefit from the same posture.

## Shared engineering principles

- define authoritative sources
- make side effects explicit
- preserve traceability
- design for imperfect downstream behavior

The implementation details differ, but the thinking does not. That is why payment-grade engineering discipline is an asset when building AI-backed products.
