## Context

Property management payments are deceptively complex because the user expects a simple experience while the platform still needs to handle reliability, compliance, and operational traceability.

This work focused on modernizing the payments foundation so residents and administrators could complete critical financial workflows with less friction and stronger system behavior under load.

## Impact

- Improved reliability and clarity across core payment workflows
- Increased delivery speed for product changes on payment-related surfaces
- Established a stronger architectural foundation for future payment capabilities

## Contribution

I drove system design across the frontend and integration layers, translated payment requirements into maintainable technical boundaries, and documented the architecture so teams could ship faster with fewer regressions.
