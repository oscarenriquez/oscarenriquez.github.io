## Context

When users cannot quickly verify payment activity, support load grows and trust drops.

This project improved the way residents and administrators consumed transactional information by focusing on speed, status clarity, and a more maintainable frontend structure.

## Impact

- Improved access to real-time transaction visibility
- Reduced friction in resident and administrator payment workflows
- Supported a more scalable frontend foundation for future platform enhancements
