## Context

The work centered on moving a rewards-oriented platform from legacy messaging into a cloud-native environment without disrupting business continuity.

The technical challenge was not only migration. It was preserving compatibility while improving throughput, reliability, and long-term maintainability.

## Impact

- Increased scalability and resilience for core event-processing flows
- Reduced dependence on legacy infrastructure
- Created a stronger foundation for future cloud-native integrations

## Contribution

I led the technical migration strategy, helped modernize the Java services involved, and ensured the transition could happen without breaking critical platform behavior.
