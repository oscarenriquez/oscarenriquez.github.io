## Context

Promotion delivery systems need to balance speed, scale, and operational reliability because they sit close to user-facing product experiences.

This work focused on building backend capabilities that could support high-volume distribution while improving response times across critical paths.

## Impact

- Supported large-scale distribution of promotions and offers
- Improved performance through stronger caching strategies
- Established a technical foundation for more personalized user experiences
