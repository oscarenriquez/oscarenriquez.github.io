## Context

This platform was created to help teams understand donation behavior across a large financial ecosystem without exposing internal business details.

The focus was not only data availability, but decision quality. The system needed to support reporting, campaign analysis, and repeatable insight generation at meaningful scale.

## Impact

- Enabled analysis of millions of donation transactions across donors, merchants, and time periods
- Improved data-driven decision making for fundraising and campaign planning
- Automated recurring reporting and dashboard delivery for internal stakeholders

## Contribution

I led the backend and data architecture, designed the BigQuery data model, and defined the multidimensional aggregation strategy that made the platform useful for analytics rather than simple storage.
