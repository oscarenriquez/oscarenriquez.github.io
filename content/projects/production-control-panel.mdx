## Context

Operational tooling has outsized impact because it improves how quickly teams understand and respond to production conditions.

This project focused on turning fragmented monitoring signals into a clearer view of workflows and infrastructure health.

## Impact

- Improved visibility into production systems and workflow behavior
- Reduced time to detect and investigate failures
- Strengthened day-to-day operational effectiveness

## Contribution

I designed the monitoring solution, built the dashboard experience, and automated parts of the operational workflow that had previously required more manual effort.
