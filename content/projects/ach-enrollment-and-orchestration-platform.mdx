## Context

ACH flows are business-critical, but they also expose the platform to latency, verification issues, and failure states that need to be handled with precision.

The objective here was not just to process payments. It was to make the lifecycle understandable, observable, and durable enough for a high-trust financial experience.

## Impact

- Strengthened reliability for ACH onboarding and payment execution
- Improved visibility into transaction states for support and operations teams
- Reduced fragility across multi-step payment workflows

I designed the orchestration model, helped define the integration boundaries, and wrote technical documentation that made the workflow easier for engineers and stakeholders to maintain over time.
